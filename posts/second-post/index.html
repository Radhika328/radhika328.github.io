Learning from Failure

February 18, 2026

In this unit, I learned how failure can teach important lessons. In society failure is seen as something negative. They think that failure means a person who is not capable or not successful. As students, we are all  afraid of failure and whenever we make any mistakes we never expect it because of shame. But this unit helped me to understand that failure is a natural part of learning. It gives us a chance to learn, improve and learn from our mistakes.

From the failure case study, I learned how failure can teach important lessons to us. The Boeing 737 MAX case showed how poor communication and small mistakes in system design lead to serious problems. The MCAS system made a big mistake, they designed a software that depended only on one sensor. Which sends wrong information and the system does not have another sensor to verify. Because of this, two tragic crashes happened, where many people lost their lives.This made me understand how important safety and communications are.

From Right Kind of Wrong: The Science of Failing Well, I learned that sometimes failures are good because they help us to learn and try new things. That can lead to an achievement also. Overall, this unit taught me that failure is not the end, but a chance to learn, improve, and make better decisions in the future.This book explains that we should study failures to understand what went wrong and how to avoid it in the future. 

Group discussion helped me to understand the failure chapter clearly, we also told each other what we know about the chapter and case study, that made things easier. Overall,this unit taught me that failure is not the end, but a chance to learn, improve, and make better decisions in the future.
