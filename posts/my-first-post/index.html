Exploring Information and Learning Through Experience

February 18, 2026

In the first unit, I learned about “What is information” studying this topic was new for me. In the beginning, I never thought about the difference between data and information. Because I used both terms in the same way. This class made me realize how often I come across data in my daily life without really thinking about how it turns into something meaningful. 

Then we learned about the DIKW model. It helped me understand how data becomes knowledge and finally into wisdom. First, it is difficult to make a difference in this topic because everything is related to each other and there is a very small difference in this topic. For example, we check weather updates on our phone. Then, seeing temperature numbers, we understand the weather pattern. Learning from it and deciding what to do is knowledge. Deciding where we have to carry an umbrella or not is wisdom. This connection made the concept more real and easier to remember.

However, I did find some parts challenging. Using tools like Zettlr and Markdown. It was not easy for me at the beginning. It was hard for me to remember the symbols and keywords when I was writing math problems.  And it is confusing also, but with the help of group members, I corrected my mistake and did that task properly. This experience taught me that learning takes time, and getting support from others can make it easier.

This made me realize that learning is not just about understanding concepts, but also about finding ways to relate them to real life.
